Our Mission

J&R is a family owned and operated estate vineyard and boutique winery on the southern coast of California that has gained esteem with our award winning red varietal wines. Our vineyard is nestled into the undulating hills of the beautiful Temecula Wine country Ranch at the eastern most edge of the Valley. The ranch and vineyard is where we, call home. We have taken great pride over the years in being stewards of the land and created wines that are unique to the beauty. On our ranch we have two regulation sized Polo fields and many powerful Polo ponies where we blend our commitment to excellence in wine with a passion for Polo.

Our Temecula Location

Our Temecula winery produces handcrafted wines that represent some of the best varietals grown in the Temecula Valley. If you are wine tasting in Temecula stop by our beautiful outdoor tasting room. J&R also has 100 olive trees which they harvest and press for fresh, delicious olive oil (available only at certain times of the year).
